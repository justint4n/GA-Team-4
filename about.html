About EatingHealthy

A simple website focused on healthy eating guidance and future chatbot support.

EatingHealthy is designed to introduce users to healthier food choices in a calm and friendly way. The site keeps the experience clean while supporting the live BotPress assistant.

Our goal

Make healthy eating feel easier to understand through a simple interface and clear content structure.

Our approach

Keep the design minimal, readable, and welcoming so users can focus on the information quickly.

What comes next

Extend the current BotPress setup with webhook-driven triggers and future content updates.

Design direction

Simple enough for first-time visitors, flexible enough for future features.

The website uses a light visual system, generous spacing, and straightforward messaging so the next development phase can focus on improving chatbot behavior instead of reworking the design.
